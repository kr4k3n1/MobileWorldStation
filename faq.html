Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a repair usually take?

Most repairs are completed within 24–48 hours, depending on the issue.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are recommended but not required. Walk-ins are welcome.

What warranty do you offer?

We offer a 3-month warranty on all repairs and parts.

Can I get a quote online?

Yes! Use the Book Repair form to describe your issue and we’ll send a quote.

What if my phone can’t be repaired?

If repair is not possible, we’ll offer a replacement or refund.

Do you sell accessories too?

Yes, we have chargers, cases, headphones, and more in our product catalog.
